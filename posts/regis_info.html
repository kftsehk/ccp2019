Registration & Participation

Registration will be done online.

Registration form collects key information e.g.

  • If one is willing to be a session chair
  • If they will submit a paper for proceedings
  • If they will be available for proceedings paper reviews
  • Dietary preferences are indicated at registration form e.g kosher, halaal and vegetarian
  • Allergies if any

4.1 Registration fees

  • Delegate Registration Fees = HK$3500 (or €430)
  • Student Registration Fees = HK$2000 (€280)
  • Day Registration Fees = HK$1500 (€200)

4.2 Travel and VISA information

Travel and VISA information will be available on the conference website.
