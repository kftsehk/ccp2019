Registration Now Opens

Updated: Jan 1, 2019

Online registration is now open. Please kindly fill the following form to register.

Deadline for registration: 17 June 2019

The registration fee for CCP2019 includes access to all scientific events, breaks and lunches, a welcome reception and banquet, and publication of an accepted manuscript.

Registration fees are:

Regular Attendees
Regular Until 17 June 2019 HK$2,000 (~USD260)
Late/On site Until 31 July 2019 HK$2,500 (~USD320)
Students and Attendees From Developing Countries†
Regular Until 17 June 2019 HK$2,000 (~USD260)
Late/On site Until 31 July 2019 HK$2,500 (~USD320)

Accompanying persons' fee 17 is HKD1200 (~USD160), and includes the welcome reception and the banquet.

† "Developing countries" are defined by the World Bank’s most recent list of "low income" and "lower middle income" economies.

Method of payment

You can pay the registration fee by credit card or bank draft payable to “The Chinese University of Hong Kong”.

For additional information or help if you meet problems when registering, please contact the conference secretariat  ccp2019@phy.cuhk.edu.hk.
