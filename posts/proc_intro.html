General information and instructions for abstract submission

Abstracts can only be submitted electronically by using the online submission system.

Contributions will be accepted either as oral presentations or posters according to the decision of the Organizing Committee. On the abstract form you can choose your presentation type preference from “talk”, “poster” or “talk or poster”. The Committee reserves the right to accept or reject abstracts.

The abstracts will be evaluated after the submission has closed on 1 May. Authors will be notified of acceptance by 15 May.

Accepted abstracts will be published in the Programme/Abstracts book, subject to the authors' confirmation and paid registration. The accepted abstracts will be published as typed by the author.

If you have any questions regarding the submission process, please contact the conference secretariat at ccp2019@phy.cuhk.edu.hk.

Format of the abstract
Download Sample Abstract

The abstract must be written in English. Please do not include any pictures or graphics at submission.

Preferred format: MS Word, One page A4, left/right/top/bottom margin: 2:54 cm, font face: Times New Roman, font size: 12 pt., single line spacing.
