CCP2019 will take place from 28 July to 1 August 2019

Events

  • Sun. 28 July 2019, Evening Welcome Reception at Chinese University of Hong Kong
  • Mon. 29 July 2019, Official Opening
  • Mon. 29 July to Thurs 1 August 2019, Scientific Programme (Plenary talks, parallel sessions and poster session)
  • Tue. 30 or Wed 31, Outreach Event at Lantau Island (see details in the later part about Excursion of the proposal)
  • Thurs. 1 August 2019, Conference Banquet with Chinese seafood is planned

Scientific Programme

The CCP2019 conference programme and format will follow recent previous CCP conferences. It will have Plenary talks, parallel sessions with invited talks and poster sessions.

Plenary and Invited Speakers

The process to identify plenary and invited speakers is currently underway. The LOC is requesting C20 to also assist in this regards

Main Topics

All areas of Computational Physics will be covered, including

  • Statistical Physics
  • Complex System and nonlinear dynamics
  • Soft Matters
  • Polymers and Biological Physics
  • Computational Biology
  • Quantum Many Body and Strongly Correlated Systems
  • Material and Nano Sciences
  • Quantum Computing
  • Astrophysics and Space Physics
  • Plasma
  • Gravitation and Cosmology
  • Atomic
  • Molecular and Optical Physics
  • High Energy
  • Nuclear and Particle physics
  • Fluid Dynamics
  • Oceanography and Climate modelling
  • Geophysics and Geomechanics
  • Computational Physics Education
  • Software and Hardware Development

Conference Proceedings

The proceedings of the CCP2019 will appear in Open Access Journal of Physics: Conference Series (JPCS), which is part of IOP Conference Series. The LOC will set up a proceedings editorial team. Papers will be accepted and reviewed using a Online system and adhering to the IOP Publishing guidelines.

Abstract Submission

Submission will be done online and submitters choose the main topic/track they want to present under, if they want oral or poster and if they have any preference on dates under general comments
