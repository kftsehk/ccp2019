31st IUPAP Conference on Computational Physics in 2019
The CCP series dates back to a conference held in Boston in 1989, which evolved into the annual EPS-APS joint conferences Physics Computing (PC). In 1997 this series was reorganized and renamed as the Conference on Computational Physics (CCP). 1st Aug. 2019 in The Chinese University of Hong Kong
Shatin, Hong Kong, China
