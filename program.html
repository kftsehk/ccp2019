Part2 Scientific Programme


The CCP2019 conference programme and format will follow recent previous CCP conferences. It will have Plenary talks, parallel sessions with invited talks and poster sessions.


2.1 Abstract Submission

Submission will be done online and submitters choose the main topic/track they want to present under, if they want oral or poster and if they have any preference on dates under general comments


2.2 Main Topics

All areas of Computational Physics will be covered, including Statistical Physics, Complex System and nonlinear dynamics, Soft Matters, Polymers and Biological Physics, Computational Biology, Quantum Many Body and Strongly Correlated Systems, Material and Nano Sciences, Quantum Computing, Astrophysics and Space Physics, Plasma, Gravitation and Cosmology, Atomic, Molecular and Optical Physics, High Energy, Nuclear and Particle physics, Fluid Dynamics, Oceanography and Climate modelling, Geophysics and Geomechanics, Computational Physics Education, Software and Hardware Development


2.3 Plenary and Invited Speakers

The process to identify plenary and invited speakers is currently underway. The LOC is requesting C20 to also assist in this regards



Part3 Conference Proceedings


The proceedings of the CCP2019 will appear in Open Access Journal of Physics: Conference Series (JPCS), which is part of IOP Conference Series. The LOC will set up a proceedings editorial team. Papers will be accepted and reviewed using a Online system and adhering to the IOP Publishing guidelines.



Part4 Registration & Participation


Registration will be done online.

  • If they will submit a paper for proceedings
